About June Coffee Shop

Learn about our story, mission, and the passionate team behind every cup

Our Story

Founded in 2025, June Coffee Shop began as a small neighborhood coffee shop with a big dream: to create a warm, welcoming space where community members could connect over exceptional coffee.

Our Mission

We are committed to serving ethically sourced, premium coffee while fostering meaningful connections within our community. Every cup we serve is a testament to our dedication to quality and sustainability.

Meet Our Team

Our passionate baristas and team members bring years of experience and genuine love for coffee to every interaction. We believe that great coffee is made by great people.
